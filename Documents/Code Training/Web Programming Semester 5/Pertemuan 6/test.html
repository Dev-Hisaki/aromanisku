About Me

  My name is Muhammad Barkah AL-Gibran, 20th years old. I am born at 02 April 2003 in Tangerang. I don't have a specific hobby, but i do playing games, and i listening to music 24/7 on Youtube. I studied Computer Engineering at Indonesian University of Education (Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia). Before that, I was graduated from SMAN 4 Tangerang at 2021 in the science class. Right now I lived at Kost Pondok Kenanga, Cinunuk.

  As a Computer Engineering student at the University of Education Indonesia, my journey throughout college has been filled with various inspiring experiences and projects. One of the most captivating and beneficial projects I have ever undertaken is the "Strawberry Fruit Sorting Based on Ripeness Level Using TCS3200 Optical Sensor." In this project, I collaborated with a team to design and implement an automated system that allows the sorting of strawberries based on their ripeness level by utilizing the TCS3200 optical sensor. We successfully created an efficient and accurate system for strawberry sorting, which positively impacted productivity and the quality of the harvest.

  I also participated in a project that combined artificial intelligence and robotics, namely the "Hexapod Robot for Victim Assistance." This robot was designed for use in remote areas or disaster-stricken areas for rescue missions. The hexapod robot was expected to navigate smoothly across challenging terrains and carry emergency supplies while assisting victims in emergency situations. This project provided profound insights into robot control algorithm development and its real-world applications. All of these experiences have enriched my understanding of computer engineering and have helped me develop strong technical skills.
